GAME RULES

Objective

Thanos: Collect all 6 Infinity Stones and Snap to win, or defeat all Heroes.

Heroes: Reduce Thanos's Life to 0 before he collects all Stones.

Game Flow

  • Thanos starts with 2 cards, Heroes with 1 card each
  • On your turn, play 1 card from your hand and use its ability
  • After playing, draw a new card if available
  • Defeated cards cost your team 1 Life
  • Use Power Tokens for +2 in fights

Card Abilities

Hero Cards:

1: Guess a number. If Thanos has it, defeat one.

2: Let another Hero peek at Thanos's hand.

3: May fight Thanos.

4: Gain 1 Power Token.

5: Look at top 3 Hero cards, rearrange them.

6: Force a Hero (or yourself) to fight Thanos.
